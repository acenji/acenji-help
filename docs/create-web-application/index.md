# 🌐 Create Web Applications

Welcome to the heart of ACENji — where you can build complete, powerful **web applications without writing code**.

Whether you want to launch a **brand-new product**, add **new features to your existing website**, or create a **secure internal system for your team**, ACENji adapts to your vision.

---

## 🧭 Choose Your Starting Point

Before you start building, think about **what you want to achieve** — ACENji supports every kind of project:

### 🧩 Build from Scratch  
Create a completely **new Web application** with its own pages, workflows, and brand identity.  
Perfect for launching new platforms, portals, or online services.

### 🧩 Add-On to an Existing Site  
Embed ACENji directly into your current website or app to **extend its capabilities**.  
You can add booking tools, dashboards, forms, or any interactive experience without disrupting your current design.

---

## 👥 Choose Your Audience

ACENji apps can serve **any type of user group** — all from the same platform:

- **Internal** — for staff, admins, or private teams behind login  
- **External** — for customers, leads, or the public  
- **Mixed** — combine internal and external users with **role-based access** and **personalized views**

> 💡 **Tip:** You don’t have to choose just one — start small, and expand your app’s audience and features over time.

---

Next, you’ll learn how to **integrate your app on your domain** and **start building with the Website Builder**.
 

## 🏠 Seamless on Your Domain

Most traditional site builders make you **start over on a new domain** — creating a separate site that feels disconnected from your brand.  
Your users have to leave your website, go to another domain, and it’s clear the experience wasn’t built in-house.

**ACENji takes the opposite approach.**

🧩 You don’t need a new domain.  
🧩 You don’t need to rebuild your existing site.  
🧩 You simply **add your new app directly into your current domain** — so it becomes part of your website from day one.

---

### ✨ How It Works

**“ACENji applications run inside your own domain — not ours.”**

Your end users will **never leave your website** — they interact with your app as if it were built by you, on your own infrastructure.

**Benefits:**

- 🪪 **Brand Trust** — Users stay on your domain, not redirected to `acenji.com` or any external platform.  
- 🕵️‍♂️ **Invisible Embedding** — Works with WordPress, HTML/JavaScript, or any site — no redesign or migration.  
- 🔐 **Secure & Isolated** — Sessions, roles, and variable-based logic are scoped to your domain for full control.  
- 🖌️ **Custom Look & Feel** — ACENji inherits your existing branding (fonts, colors, headers) for a seamless user experience.

> 💡 Start with a single page or feature inside your site. Expand as you grow — all on your existing domain.
 

## ⚙️ Integration Options

ACENji is designed to **fit into what you already have** — whether that’s a full WordPress site or a simple HTML/JavaScript page.  
You don’t have to start over or hire a development team.  

There are two easy ways to bring your new app to life:

---

### 🧩 WordPress Integration  
If your website is built on **WordPress**, ACENji can plug right in.  
Use our free plugin to embed your application directly inside your existing pages — no code, no disruption.

- Keep your current design and content  
- Add interactive ACENji apps alongside your existing pages  
- Manage everything from your WordPress dashboard

> 💡 Users will never notice it’s a separate application — it looks and feels like part of your site.

---

### ✂️ HTML/JavaScript Integration  
If your site is **custom-built or uses plain HTML/JavaScript**, you can still add ACENji easily.  
Just paste a small snippet of code where you want the app to appear — it launches inside your layout without changing your design.

- No need to rebuild or redesign  
- Works with any hosting platform  
- Add to one page or many

---

### 🤝 We Can Help (Optional)  
Not sure which option is right for you?  
No problem — our team can handle the entire setup **from A to Z**:

- Connecting your domain  
- Installing the WordPress plugin or HTML snippet  
- Configuring your first app  
- Making sure it matches your branding perfectly

> 💬 Just [contact us](https://www.acenji.com/contact) and tell us about your site — we’ll help you get it running smoothly.

## 🧰 What ACENji Web Applications Can Do

ACENji gives you everything you need to build **real, multi-user web applications** — not just static pages.  
It’s more than a site builder — it’s a full platform for building interactive systems with users, permissions, logic, and automation.

Here’s what makes it powerful:

---

### 🔐 Secure Logins & User Roles  
Give people their own accounts to log in to your app.  
You control exactly **who can access what** by assigning **roles** (like Admin, Manager, Member) and managing **individual permissions**.

- Restrict certain pages or features to specific roles  
- Show or hide content based on who is logged in  
- Personalize experiences for each user

---

### 👥 Groups & Timed Sessions  
Organize users into **groups** and control **when they can access your app**.  
For example, let one group in this week, and another group next week — all automatically.

- Schedule start and end dates for group access  
- Run group sessions, courses, or cohorts  
- Track who belongs to which group at any time

---

### 📊 Smart Variables & Dynamic Content  
Use ACENji’s **Variable Center** to store information about users or sessions and make your app respond to it in real time.

- Display different content based on user data  
- Turn features on/off dynamically  
- Trigger workflows based on variable values

---

### ⚡ Conditional Logic & Page Control  
Control **which parts of a page are visible or interactive** based on conditions.

- Show or hide sections, tiles, or even individual elements  
- Create multi-step flows, gated pages, or time-locked content  
- Build interactive dashboards and workflows without code

---

### 🧱 Visual Builder & Event-Driven Design  
Build everything using a **drag-and-drop visual builder** — no coding needed.  
Every element can respond to user actions like clicks, submissions, or selections.

- Design your pages visually  
- Link actions to triggers (e.g. “When user clicks, go to next page”)  
- Build forms, dashboards, portals, and more

---

> 💡 In short: ACENji lets you build **secure, dynamic, user-aware applications** — visually, and without writing a single line of code.
